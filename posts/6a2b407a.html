影响谷歌排名的200个因素

2014-03-27

作者：**Brian Dean** 最后一次更新时间：**2014.2.26** 原文网址：http://backlinko.com/google-ranking-factors 感谢:程远宇供稿

也许你已经知道在谷歌算法中涉及到了约**200个排名因素，但是它们到底是什么？今天你会惊喜因为我已经把它们都列在了一起：有些已被证实，有些还在争议，有些只是SEO**书呆子们的猜测。但是今天它们都在这了。

一 域名因素

1. Domain Age: In this video, Matt Cutts states that:“The difference between a domain that’s six months old verses one year old is really not that big at all.”In other words, they do use domain age…but it’s not very important.

1 。域名年龄：在一个视频中，**Matt Cutts说：“一个域名是半岁或是一岁，之间的区别真的不是那么大的。”换句话说，他们确实使用域名年龄作为网站的排名依据……**但它不是很重要。

2. Keyword Appears in Top Level Domain: Doesn’t give the boost that it used to, but having your keyword in the domain still acts as a relevancy signal. After all, they still bold keywords that appear in a domain name.

2 。顶级域名中出现关键词：这个的影响程度已经没有以前深了，特别是**EMD**更新之后。但是可以保证这仍然是一个比较重要的因素。

3. Keyword As First Word in Domain: SEOMoz’s 2011 Search Engine Ranking Factors panelists agreed that a domain that starts with their target keyword has an edge over sites that either don’t have the keyword in their domain or have the keyword in the middle or end of their domain.

3 。以关键词开头的域名： SEOmoz 2011**每两年一度的Google排名影响因素调查报告中明确表示，目标关键字在域名开头的网站比要么站点域名中未包含关键词或关键词在域名中间亦或是结尾的网站更具备优势。**

4. Domain registration length: A Google patent states: “Valuable (legitimate) domains are often paid for several years in advance, while doorway (illegitimate) domains rarely are used for more than a year. Therefore, the date when a domain expires in the future can be used as a factor in predicting the legitimacy of a domain”.

4 。域名登记时长：一份谷歌专利声明中称：**“有价值的（正规的）域名往往提前支付数年的域名使用权，而门域（doorway domain**：专为提高特定关键词在搜索引擎中的排名所设计的富含目标关键词的域名，然后重定向至其它域名的主页。）使用时间很少超过一年。因此，一个域名未来的使用年限将是审视网站价值的因素之一。

5. Keyword in Subdomain Name: SEOMoz’s panel also agreed that a keyword appearing in the subdomain boosts rank.

5 。关键字出现在子域名中： 同样是**SEOMoz**的调查报告中指出，某个关键词出现在子域名中有助于提高该网站排名。

6. Domain History: A site with volatile ownership (via whois) or several drops may tell Google to “reset” the site’s history, negating links pointing to the domain.

6 。域名历史：一个网站反复的更换一个或多个所有者（通过**whoiS查询）可能会告诉谷歌“重置”**网站历史，所有指向此域名的链接视为无效。

7. Exact Match Domain: EMDs may still give you an edge…if it’s a quality site. But if the EMD happens to be a low-quality site, it’s vulnerable to the EMD update:

7 。完全匹配域名（**EMD）：完全匹配域名可能依然会给你的网站排名带来一些优势……前提是网站是高质量网站。但是，如果EMD**恰好配上一个低质量的网站，它的作用往往适得其反。

8. Public vs. Private WhoIs: Private WhoIs information may be a sign of “something to hide”. Matt Cutts is quoted as stating at Pubcon 2006: “…When I checked the whois on them, they all had “whois privacy protection service” on them. That’s relatively unusual. …Having whois privacy turned on isn’t automatically bad, but once you get several of these factors all together, you’re often talking about a very different type of webmaster than the fellow who just has a single site or so.”

8 。公开与隐藏**WHOIS** ：隐藏**WHOIS信息可能意味着“有一些东西要掩盖的”。Matt Cutts曾在Pubcon 2006峰会上说过：“ ……当我检查他们的whois信息时，他们都有“WHOIS隐私保护服务”**在他们身上。这是非常不寻常的。 …**开启WHOIS隐私保护服务不见得是坏事，但是当我们发现你的网站也有一些其他不自然的表现时，这所有的东西综合起来可以让我们相信你的网站的信任度是有问题的。**”

9. Penalized WhoIs Owner: If Google identifies a particular person as a spammer it makes sense that they would scrutinize other sites owned by that person.

9 。受过惩罚的**WHOIS所有者：如果google**认定一个特定的人是黑帽站长，这意味着他们也会审查该人名下所拥有的其他网站。

10. Country TLD extension: Having a Country Code Top Level Domain (.cn, .pt, .ca) helps the site rank for that particular country…but limits the site’s ability to rank globally.

10 。国家顶级域名：具有国家代码的顶级域名（**.CN** ，**.PT** ，**.CA** ），有助于网站在特定国家的排名**……**但它也限制了网站在全球的排名能力。

二、**页面因素**

11. Keyword in Title Tag: The title tag is a webpage’s second most important piece of content (besides the content of the page) and therefore sends a strong on-page SEO signal.

11**。标题标签包含关键字：标题标签是网页第二个最重要的内容（仅次于页面本身内容），它发出了强大的网页搜索引擎优化信号。**

12. Title Tag Starts with Keyword: According to SEOMoz data, title tags that starts with a keyword tend to perform better than title tags with the keyword towards the end of the tag:

12**。关键词在标题标签中的位置：还是根据之前SEOMoz的报告，让关键词出现在title标签开头会比让关键词出现在中间甚至末尾具有更好的SEO效果。**

13. Keyword in Description Tag: Another important relevancy signal.

13. 关键词是否出现在**description标签：原理和title标签一样，只是重要程度比title**略低。

14. Keyword Appears in H1 Tag: H1 tags are a “second title tag” that sends another relevancy signal to Google, according to results from this correlation study:

14**。关键词是否出现在H1标签：H1标签可以看作是“第二title”，从这个称号就可以看到重要性了。**

15. Keyword is Most Frequently Used Phrase in Document: Having a keyword appear more than any other likely acts as a relevancy signal.

15**。** 关键词是否是全文使用频率最高的词：理论上来讲突出使用关键词会让**Google更容易判断你这个页面的keyword，（翻译者认为：但是我个人来讲不主张刻意这样。让关键词出现在title, description, h1, h2**，替换文本，然后文中就不用刻意出现关键词了。毕竟你这个页面的中心是这个词，正常行文的时候不可能不用到这个词。但是如果行文的时候脑子里一直是这个词，那就很可能无意识地就造成关键词堆砌了。）

16. Content Length: Content with more words can cover a wider breadth and are likely preferred to shorter superficial articles. SERPIQ found that content length correlated with SERP position.

16**。文章长度:文章更长的话肯定可以覆盖到的内容更多，也就更能解决用户的问题及需求。一项调查发现SERP排前3的页面平均长度超过2400个词，后面4-10位呈递减状态，第十的也有2000词。（翻译者补充：这里我个人也要补充一下：不要盲目拉长文章。一定要保证你的文章内容和中心关键词息息相关，如果为了拉长文章，或者处于解决用户需求的考虑，添加了一些没有太大相关的内容，可能会对Google解析索引你的页面造成影响。实在需要补充一些东西，而且篇幅略长的话，宁愿另外开一个页面，然后相互链接。）**

17. Keyword Density: Although not as important as it once was, keyword density is still something Google uses to determine the topic of a webpage. But going overboard can hurt you.

17**。关键词密度：虽然不像以前一样重要,关键字密度仍然是google用来判断一个网页的主题因素。但死钻牛角尖肯定会事倍功半。**

18. Latent Semantic Indexing Keywords in Content (LSI): LSI keywords help search engines extract meaning from words with more than one meaning (Apple the computer company vs. the fruit). The presence/absence of LSI probably also acts as a content quality signal.

18**。文章中的潜在语义索引关键词：潜在语音索引即Latent Semantic Indexing，是搜索引擎语义理解范畴内的概念。如文中出现Apple到底是指苹果公司还是水果？让文章的内容有助于Google理解这些LSI关键词，也许会让Google给予你的内容更高的质量评分。**

19. LSI Keywords in Title and Description Tags: As with webpage content, LSI keywords in page meta tags probably help Google discern between synonyms. May also act as a relevancy signal.

19**。Title和Description里面是否出现LSI关键词：除了文章主体内容之外，meta标签里面的LSI关键词也很可能会对你的排名有一定影响。（翻译者补充：总而言之，不要对Google理解你的内容造成麻烦，尽量帮助Google更准确地理解你的内容。）**

20. Page Loading Speed via HTML: Both Google and Bing use page loading speed as a ranking factor. Search engine spiders can estimate your site speed fairy accurately based on a page’s code and filesize.

20**。页面载入速度：Google和Bing都明确表示页面载入速度是排名因素之一，根据页面代码和文件的大小蜘蛛会公平准确的判断页面的打开速度。（翻译者补充：所以尽量精简代码，必要的时候使用CDN。）**

21. Duplicate Content: Identical content on the same site (even slightly modified) can negatively influence a site’s search engine visibility.

21**。重复内容：页面上出现重复内容（甚至轻微模仿）肯定会对排名有负面影响。**

22. Rel=Canonical: When used properly, use of this tag may prevent Google from considering pages duplicate content.

22**。Rel=Canonical标签：合理使用这个标签也许可以阻止谷歌认为页面内容重复。**

23. Page Loading Speed via Chrome: Google may also use Chrome user data to get a better handle on a page’s loading time as this takes into account server speed, CDN usage and other non HTML-related site speed signals.

23**。Chrome下的页面载入速度：Google会使用Chrome使用者的资料来更准确地判定你的页面载入速度，包括服务器链接速度，是否使用CDN，和一些其他与HTML代码无关的一些载入速度因素。**

24. Image Optimization: Images on-page send search engines important relevancy signals through their file name, alt text, title, description and caption.

24**。图片优化：一定的图片优化可以对SEO有正面影响，包括图片的文件名，替换文本，标题，描述和说明。**

25. Recency of Content Updates: Google Caffeine update favors recently updated content, especially for time-sensitive searches. Highlighting this factor’s importance, Google shows the date of a page’s last update for certain pages:

25**。最近的内容更新：Google的官方博客指出页面最近更新时间是他们考虑的参数之一，特别针对一些时效性的关键词，最近更新的内容会比较受青睐。（翻译者补充：从SERP页面也可以直接看到页面更新时间）**

26. Magnitude of Content Updates: The significance of edits and changes is also a freshness factor. Adding or removing entire sections is a more significant update than switching around the order of a few words.

26**。页面更新的程度：对页面的修改/更新程度也是页面新鲜度的一个标准之一。大面积的增减或者编辑全文，会比小范围的修改的影响更大。（翻译者补充：不管是好的影响还是坏的影响，我觉得这个有点凑数嫌疑）**

27. Historical Updates Page Updates: How often has the page been updated over time? Daily, weekly? Frequency of page updates also play a role in freshness.

27**。页面的历史更新记录：这个页面一直以来的更新频率怎样？每天？每周？更新频率同样在新鲜度的评分里面有一席之地。**

28. Keyword Prominence: Having a keyword appear in the first 100-words of a page’s content appears to be a significant relevancy signal.

28**。关键词是否突出：让关键词出现在文章的前100词里面会比让关键词出现在后面更有效果。**

29. Keyword in H2, H3 Tags: Having your keyword appear as a subheading in H2 or H3 format may be another weak relevancy signal. SEOMoz’s panel agrees.

29**。关键词是否出现在H2, H3标签：也许是另一个相对较弱的相关因素，SEOMoz的报告里也提到了。（翻译者补充：和H1标签一样，不赘述了。）**

30. Keyword Word Order: An exact match of a searcher’s keyword in a page’s content will generally rank better than the same keyword phrase in a different order. For example: consider a search for: “cat shaving techniques”. A page optimized for the phrase “cat shaving techniques” will rank better than a page optimized for “techniques for shaving a cat”.

30**。关键词的词语顺序：当用户搜”google seo tips”的时候，专门优化过”google seo tips”会比优化“seo tips for google”的网页有更多加分。**

31. Outbound Link Quality: Many SEOs think that linking out to authority sites helps send trust signals to Google.

31**，网站输出的链接质量：很多SEO人员相信导出到高信任度的权威网站有助于提升自己网站的权威度。**

32. Outbound Link Theme: According to SEOMoz, search engines may use the content of the pages you link to as a relevancy signal. For example, if you have a page about cars that links to movie-related pages, this may tell Google that your page is about the movie Cars, not the automobile.

32**。导出链接目标网站的内容主题：还是根据SEOMoz的报告，搜索引擎会根据你导出链接的目标网站的内容作为判定你网页内容的依据之一。譬如，如果你有一个关于车的页面链接到电影相关的页面，谷歌可能会认为你的网页时讲述与电影车有关的内容而不是汽车。**

33. Grammar and Spelling: Proper grammar and spelling is a quality signal, although Cutts gave mixed messages in 2011 on whether or not this was important.

33**。语法和拼写：正确的语法和拼写是你的文章质量的重要标准，Matt Cutts公开声明过这一点。**

34. Syndicated Content: Is the content on the page original? If it’s scraped or copied from an indexed page it won’t rank as well as the original or end up in their Supplemental Index.

34**。内容抄袭：你网页的内容是你原创吗？还是采集或抄袭的那些排名不是太好(甚至被纳入到“Supplemental Index补充索引”里的)的网站上的内容？如果非原创，你的排名不会比这些内容的出处好或者没有排名。** （翻译者补充：古老的名词，有兴趣的可以去搜一下什么是**Supplemental Index，可以诶追溯到2003年的一个SEO名词。)**

35. Helpful Supplementary Content: According to a now-public Google Rater Guidelines Document, helpful supplementary content is an indicator of a page’s quality (and therefore, Google ranking). Examples include currency converters, loan interest calculators and interactive recipes.

35**。在Supplemental Index里的有价值的内容：根据泄露出来的Google质量评分操作手册，Supplemental Index里的有价值的内容会对网站整体质量有帮助。(当然会对谷歌排名有帮助)举个例子比如货币转换器页面，贷款利率计算器页面，等等。**

36. Number of Outbound Links: Too many dofollow OBLs may “leak” PageRank, which can hurt search visibility.

36**。导出链接数量：太多的导出链接数量可能会造成PR值外泄进而影响搜索展现。（翻译者补充：这也是为什么在做外链的时候如果这个页面上太多导出链接，那你做了也白做。一是蜘蛛爬不了那么远，二是等爬到你了也没有PR值可以传递了）。**

37. Multimedia: Images, videos and other multimedia elements may act as a content quality signal.

37**。多媒体：页面上的图片，音频，视频等等都有助于页面的质量评分。**

38. Number of Internal Links Pointing to Page: The number of internal links to a page indicates its importance relative to other pages on the site.

38**。指向这个页面的内链数量：从这个参数可以看到这个页面在网站内的重要程度。**

39. Quality of Internal Links Pointing to Page: Internal links from authoritative pages on domain have a stronger effect than pages with no or low PR.

29**。指向这个页面的内链质量：从比较重要权威的（PR值高的）内部页面链接向这个页面，优化效果更好。**

40. Broken Links: Having too many broken links on a page may be a sign of a neglected or abandoned site. The Google Rater Guidelines Document uses broken links as one was to assess a homepage’s quality.

40**。死链：页面上的死链太多绝对不是好事，谷歌操作手册中死链数量是衡量页面质量的一个因素。（翻译者补充：学会使用站长工具和Xenu检测死链可以解决这一问题。）**

41. Reading Level: There’s no doubt that Google estimates the reading level of webpages, But what they do with that information is up for debate. Some say that a basic reading level will help your page rank because it will appeal to the masses. However, Linchpin SEO discovered that reading level was one factor that separated quality sites from content mills.

41**。易读性：毫无疑问Google会把易读性也考虑到排名因素里面去。大部分的SEO认为让文章尽量易读是获得收录及排名的前提，也有一部分人认为提高文章的阅读深度是高质量内容的表现。**

42. Affiliate Links: Affiliate links themselves probably won’t hurt your rankings. But if you have too many, Google’s algorithm may pay closer attention to other quality signals to make sure you’re not a “thin affiliate site”.

42**。代理链接：代理链接本身不会对你的排名造成副作用，但是如果数量过于庞大，你也许会引起Google的注意让他们重新审视你的网站质量是以确定你不是一个单薄的附属链接网址。**

43. HTML errors/WC3 validation: Lots of HTML errors or sloppy coding may be a sign of a poor quality site. While controversial, many in SEO think that WC3 validation is a weak quality signal.

43**。代码错误/W3C验证：过多的不规范甚至错误的HTML代码会让你的网页给Google造成低质量的印象。同时，这也是有争议的，许多Seoer认为WC3验证影响没那么大。（翻译者补充：请善于利用W3C的验证工具。现在浏览器的纠错能力都比较强，网页正常跑不代表代码没有问题。）**

44. Page Host’s Domain Authority: All things being equal a page on an authoritative domain will higher than a page on a domain with less authority.

44**。页面所在域名的权重：一个权威网站的页面价值比一个不够权威网站的页面价值要高。（翻译者补充：这个不难理解，比如一个常识：从微软的官网上一个PR3的页面获得一个外链，比在一个小网站PR4的页面获得的外链更有价值。）**

45. Page’s PageRank: Not perfectly correlated. But in general higher PR pages tend to rank better than low PR pages.

45**。页面的PR：虽然PR值已经不如以前那么重要，但是平均来看高PR值的页面还是比较容易获得排名。**

46. URL Length: Search Engine Journal notes that excessively long URLs may hurt search visibility.

46**。URL长度：搜搜引擎杂志提出太长的URL会对你的排名造成副作用。**

47. URL Path: A page closer to the homepage may get a slight authority boost.

47**。URL的路径：距离主页越近的URL权重也就越高。**

48. Human Editors: Although never confirmed, Google has filed a patent for a system that allows human editors to influence the SERPs.

48**。人工干预：虽然从未获得官方确认，但是Google的一份文件还是表明会有人工对SERP进行干预的可能。**

49. Page Category: The category the page appears on is a relevancy signal. A page that’s part of a closely related category should get a relevancy boost compared to a page that’s filed under an unrelated or less related category.

49**。页面目录：现在很多页面在SERP里面的标题下面（取代URL的位置）可以看到页面所属目录。如果这个页面与这个目录的相关度比较高，就比较容易在相关关键词的SERP里面获得较高排名。**

50. WordPress Tags: Tags are WordPress-specific relevancy signal. “The only way it improves your SEO is by relating one piece of content to another, and more specifically a group of posts to each other”

50**。WordPress的标签：WordPress网站的标签页对于相关度来说是一个比较重要的信号。**

51. Keyword in URL: Another important relevancy signal.

51**。关键词出现在url中：上一篇文章提到了出现在域名中的重要性，如果关键词无法出现在域名中，请一定确保他能出现在URL中。**

52. URL String: The categories in the URL string are read by Google and may provide a thematic signal to what a page is about:

52**，URL的字符：对于URL中的字符Google也会读取其意义并且作为这个页面归类索引的参考之一。**

53. References and Sources: Citing references and sources, like research papers do, may be a sign of quality. The Google Quality Guidelines states that reviewers should keep an eye out for sources when looking at certain pages: “This is a topic where expertise and/or authoritative sources are important…”.

53**，参考来源：对于引用的内容给出参考来源有助于增加页面质量评分。Google的质量评分操作手册有讲到这一点。**

54. Bullets and Numbered Lists: Bullets and numbered lists help break up your content for readers, making them more user friendly. Google likely agrees and may prefer content with bullets and numbers.

54**，有序/无序列表：使用有序/无序列表可以帮助Google更好地分割你的内容从而更容易理解。而且这样的内容对于用户的阅读也更友好。**

55. Priority of Page in Sitemap: The priority a page is given via the sitemap.xml file may influence ranking.

55**，页面在Sitemap中的权重：这个无需解释，知道Sitemap是什么的都能理解。**

56. Too Many Outbound Links: Straight from the aforementioned Quality rater document:“Some pages have way, way too many links, obscuring the page and distracting from the Main Content”

56**，太多的出站链接:直接引述Google质量评定报告的一段话:“一些页面**, 太多的链接**,会造成模糊的页面和分散的主题”**。

57. Quantity of Other Keywords Page Ranks For: If the page ranks for several other keywords it may give Google an internal sign of quality.

57**。页面的关键词数量：这里的关键词数量是指被索引到而且获得排名的关键词，即可以搜到你这个页面的关键词。这个数量越多Google会认为你的页面质量更高。（长尾关键词）**

58. Page Age: Although Google prefers fresh content, an older page that’s regularly updated may outperform a newer page.

58**。页面年龄：虽然Google更喜欢新鲜的内容，但是新鲜的内容也可以出现在一个比较有历史的页面上面。原理和域名年龄相同。**

59. User Friendly Layout: Citing the Google Quality Guidelines Document yet again:

“The page layout on highest quality pages makes the Main Content immediately visible”

59**。排版：这个非常重要，是Google对页面质量评分的重要参数。去年Google的一次更新就是针对页面的排版，如果第一屏看不到什么正文，只有各种Banner和广告，那么你可能会收到惩罚。**

60. Parked Domains: A Google update in December of 2011 decreased search visibility of parked domains.

60**。闲置域名：对于闲置域名的定义是：注册后没进行使用，或者是只放了少量内容（且通常伴随广告），或者是长期不更新的域名。Google在2011年11月的更新历史中明确表示：对于闲置的域名我们倾向于不把他显示在SERP中。**

三、**网站层级因素**

61. Content Provides Value and Unique Insights: Google has stated that they’re on the hunt for sites that don’t bring anything new or useful to the table, especially thin affiliate sites.

61**。** 内容是否有价值，是否独到：**Google**在他们的帮助文档中明确表示他们会注意那些内容毫无价值毫无新意的网站，特别是代理站点。（也可以理解为站群，联署计划等）

62. Contact Us Page: The aforementioned Google Quality Document states that they prefer sites with an “appropriate amount of contact information”. Supposed bonus if your contact information matches your whois info.

62**。“联系我们”页面：前面多次提到的页面质量评分手册里面说到Google比较偏爱那些提供了尽量多且准确的联系信息的网站。而且会与Whois的信息进行匹配。**

63. Domain Trust/TrustRank: Site trust — measured by how many links away your site is from highly-trusted seed sites — is a massively important ranking factor. You can read more about TrustRank here.

63**。域名信任度：域名的信任度主要靠高质量的信任外链来源，关于信任排名(Trust Rank)的更多信息可以参考这个页面。**

64. Site Architecture: A well put-together site architecture (especially a silo structure) helps Google thematically organize your content.

64**。站点结构：良好的站点结构可以帮助Google更好地理解并组织你的内容。**

65. Site Updates: How often a site is updated — and especially when new content is added to the site — is a site-wide freshness factor.

65**。站点更新频率：站点的更新频率，特别添加新内容的频率是整站的一个新鲜度指标。**

66. Number of Pages: The number of pages a site is a weak sign of authority. At the very least a large site helps distinguish it from thin affiliate sites.

66**。页面数量：这个指标其实影响可以忽略不计，主要是帮助Google识别真正有质量的站点和劣质的代理网站。**

67. Presence of Sitemap: A sitemap helps search engines index your pages easier and more thoroughly, improving visibility.

67**。站点地图（Sitemap）：如果你的网站没有一个组织良好的站点地图，那快去补课吧，这个是基础中的基础。**

68. Site Uptime: Lots of downtime from site maintenance or server issues may hurt your ranking (and can even result in deindexing if not corrected).

68**。网站服务器是否稳定：一个经常宕机无法访问的网站Google肯定是不喜欢的。在2011年的时候我公司的网站由于服务器宕机20多个小时，恢复后流量直接少了三分之一。**

69. Server Location: Server location may influence where your site ranks in different geographical regions. Especially important for geo-specific searches.

69**。服务器位置：这个主要影响在特定搜索引擎中的排名，比如你的目标是google.fr，那么把服务器放到法国肯定会有加分。**

70. SSL Certificate (Ecommerce Sites): Google has confirmed that they index SSL certificates. It stands to reason that they’ll preferentially rank ecommerce sites with SSL certificates.

70**。SSL证书：这个仅仅针对电子商务网站，而且Google也明确表示他们会收录SSL证书。**

71. Terms of Service and Privacy Pages: These two pages help tell Google that a site is a trustworthy member of the internet.

71**。服务条款和隐私政策页面：这两个页面也是你的网站是否正规可信的参考依据。**

72. Duplicate Content On-Site: Duplicate pages and meta information across your site may bring down all of your page’s visibility.

72**。网站是否有重复内容：对于重复内容，Google只会选取其中的一个版本进行收录。**

73. Breadcrumb Navigation: This is a style of user-friendly site-architecture that helps users (and search engines) know where they are on a site:Both SearchEngineJournal.com and Ethical SEO Consulting claim that this set-up may be a ranking factor.

73**。面包屑导航：用户友好的网站结构必须要有面包屑。**

74. Mobile Optimized: Google’s official stance on mobile is to create a responsive site. It’s likely that responsive sites get an edge in searches from a mobile device.

74**。是否为移动平台进行优化：未来是移动互联网的时代，所以让你的网站更适应移动平台是将来的必修课。用平板和手机访问网站用户看到的会是不同排版。**

75. YouTube: There’s no doubt that YouTube videos are given preferential treatment in the SERPs (probably because Google owns it ):In fact, Search Engine Land found that YouTube.com traffic increased significantly after Google Panda.

75. YouTube:**不能质疑的是YouTube视频在google搜索排名中的优惠待遇(可能是因为谷歌拥有它):事实上,不难发现，在Google推出熊猫算法之后，youtube的流量和关键词排名有了显著提升。**

76. Site Usability: A site that’s difficult to use or to navigate can hurt ranking by reducing time on site, pages viewed and bounce rate. This may be an independent algorithmic factor gleaned from massive amounts of user data.

76**。站点用户体验：一个站点如果结构混乱，导航混乱，排版混乱。那结果必须是跳出率高，在线时间短，PV低。**

77. Use of Google Analytics and Google Webmaster Tools: Some think that having these two programs installed on your site can improve your page’s indexing. They may also directly influence rank by giving Google more data to work with (ie. more accurate bounce rate, whether or not you get referall traffic from your backlinks etc.).

77**。是否使用Google Analytics和Google Webmaster Tools：有些观点说Google会考虑这2个因素，和影响排名相比，更大的作用是影响收录。**

78. User reviews/Site reputation: A site’s on review sites like Yelp.com and RipOffReport.com likely play an important role in the algorithm. Google even posted a rarely candid outline of their approach to user reviews after an eyeglass site was caught ripping off customers in an effort to get backlinks.

** 78。用户评价：站点在其他网站的评价，如Yelp, RifOffReport, WOT等，也会影响网站的排名。因为这些评分站点Google**也是会去爬行，索引并读取内容的。而且这些网站往往是信任度非常 高的，所以悉心维护你的网站的评分也是很有必要的。

四、**反向链接因素**

79. Linking Domain Age: Backlinks from aged domains may be more powerful than new domains.

79. 外链域名年龄：从比较老的域名来的外链相对来说更有价值。

80. # of Linking Root Domains: The number of referring domains is one of the most important ranking factors in Google’s algorithm, as you can see from this chart from SEOMoz(bottom axis is SERPposition).

80. 外链域名数量：外链域名数量甚至比单纯的外链数量更具有影响力，根据**SEOMoz的统计，在Google排前2位的网页平均有接近800个外链域名，而第三就平均只有200**个了。

81. # of Links from Separate C-Class IPs: Links from seperate class-c IP addresses suggest a wider breadth of sites linking to you.

81. C类IP**上的链接：C类IP的定义请参照百度百科，而C类IP来的外链越多，分布越广，说明你的网站越受欢迎。**

82. # of Linking Pages: The total number of linking pages — even if some are on the same domain — is a ranking factor.

82. 外链数量：即使是同一域名上的外链，数量越多也会对你网站帮助越大。但是注意，如果你的绝大多数外链都是来自那几个网站，这样是不健康不自然的信号。

83. Alt Tag (for Image Links): Alt text is an image’s version of anchor text.

83.**图片外链的替换文本：对于图片外链来说，替换文本就是锚文本了。**

84. Links from .edu or .gov Domains: Matt Cutts has stated that TLD doesn’t factor into a site’s importance. However, that doesn’t stop SEOs from thinking that there’s a special place in the algo for .gov and .edu TLDs.

84. 从**.edu和.gov域名来的外链：Matt Cutts说顶级域名不是网页排名的参考因素。但是鉴于之前说的他的话不能全听，而且从我接触到的一些黑帽案例来讲，.edu**域名来的外链还是很有效果 的，**.gov**外链难度太大，接触到的案例比较少。

85. PR of Linking Page: The PageRank of the referring page is an extremely important ranking factor.

85. 外链页面的**PR值：这个做SEO的都知道了，从高PR**值页面来的外链会比较有价值。

86. Authority of Linking Domain: The referring domain’s authority may play an independent role in a link’s importance (ie. a PR2 page link from a site with a homepage PR3 may be worth less than a PR2 page link from PR8 Yale.edu).

86. 外链域名的权威度：从一个首页**PR为8的网站中的PR3页面来的外链，会比一个首页PR为3的网站中的PR4**页面来的外链更有价值。

87. Links From Competitors: Links from other pages ranking in the same SERP may be more valuable for a page’s rank for that particular keyword.

87. 从竞争对手来的外链：从同一关键词的竞争对手的网站上来的外链，会比其他网站来的外链更有价值。

88. Social Shares of Referring Page: The amount of page-level social shares may influence the link’s value.

88. 社交网络上的分享次数：一个页面如果在社交网站上分享的次数越多，对这个页面的排名也就越有帮助。

89. Links from Bad Neighborhoods: Links from “bad neighborhoods” may hurt your site.

89. 从**“坏邻居”来的外链：“坏邻居”就是在同一个IP段上有一个Google认为的垃圾网站，那么这个IP**段上的其他网站也会受波及。那么如果你从这些网站上获取外链，那么你的网站也会有负面影响。

90. Guest Posts: Although definitely white hat SEO, links coming from guest posts — especially in an author bio area — may not be as valuable as a contextual link on the same page.

90. Guest Blog**：通过给博客投稿的方式，在作者介绍区域获得外链，没有在正文中的外链那么有价值。而且业界现在对于投稿获得的外链持担心态度，因为自从企鹅算法** 1.0**后，这几乎成了** 最主要的外链获取手段，从**Google的习惯来看，如果大家都通过这种方法获取外链，那么这种方法即将成为被打击的对象了。当然Google**也不是不分青红皂白，如果你投稿的目标网站是很有 权威度的博客，而且你的文章质量够高，我相信是不会受影响的。

91. Links to Homepage Domain that Page Sits On: Links to a referring page’s homepage may play special importance in evaluating a site’s — and therefore a link’s — weight.

91. 从首页获取的外链：一般来讲，首页外链比内页外链更有价值。

92. Nofollow Links: One of the most controversial topics in SEO. Google’s official word on the matter is:“In general, we don’t follow them.”Which suggests that they do…at least in certain cases. Having a certain % of nofollow links may also indicate a natural vs. unnatural link profile.

92. Nofollow**外链：有一定数量的Nofollow外链是很正常的，因为当你的外链足够多，不可能没人在博客留言区或者论坛之类的地方提到你的网站，这些地方的链接往往都是nofollow。如果** 你的外链里面几乎没有**nofollow，Google**绝对有理由怀疑你的外链来源及获取手段不正常。

93. Diversity of Link Types: Having an unnaturally large percentage of your links come from a single source (ie. forum profiles, blog comments) may be a sign of webspam. On the other hand, links from diverse sources is a sign of a natural link profile.

93. 外链类型：外链类型尽量多样化，有友链，论坛，博客，正文，作者介绍区域，账号档案等等。如果你的外链类型太单一，这也是不自然的标志。

94. “Sponsored Links” Or Other Words Around Link: Words like “sponsors”, “link partners” and “sponsored links” may decrease a link’s value.

94. “**赞助链接”：“赞助链接”已经被Google认为是购买的链接了，Google是会忽略掉这种链接的。而且如果你的外链周围出现了“赞助”，“合作”，“伙伴”等字眼，Google也会怀疑** 这个链接是购买的性质。

95. Contextual Links: Links embedded inside a page’s content are considered more powerful than links on an empty page or found elsewhere on the page. A good example of contextual links are backlinks from guestographics.

95. 文本链接：即正文中的带锚文本的链接，毫无疑问这是最有价值的外链，但前提是你得文章是原创且高质量的。

96. Excessive 301 Redirects to Page: Links coming from 301 redirects dilute some (or even all) PR, according to a Webmaster Help Video.

96. 外链来源页面涉及到**301跳转：由于这是一种比较常见的劫持PR的黑帽手段，所以从301**页面来的外链往往没有价值。

97. Backlink Anchor Text: As noted in this description of Google’s original algorithm:

“First, anchors often provide more accurate descriptions of web pages than the pages themselves.”

Obviously, anchor text is less important than before (and likely a webspam signal). But it still sends a strong relevancy signal in small doses.

97. 锚文本：这条因素在**google的原始算法中被提到：“首先,** 锚往往提供的网页描述比自己的网页本身所描述的信息更准确。**”显然在当下，锚文本文本不像以前那么重要(更可能是个垃圾链接的标识)**。但它在一定程度上仍然发出了一个强烈的相关性信号。

98. Internal Link Anchor Text: Internal link anchor text is another relevancy signal, although probably weighed differently than backlink anchor text.

98. 内链锚文本：虽然作用不如外链的锚文本大，但是仍然有作用。

99. Link Title Attribution: The link title (the text that appears when you hover over a link) is also used as a weak relevancy signals.

99. 链接的**title**属性：链接标题（当你在链接上悬停时出现的文字）也被用来作为一个弱相关性的标识。

100. Country TLD of Referring Domain: Getting links from country-specific top level domain extensions (.de, .cn, .co.uk) may help you rank better in that country.

100. 国家顶级域名外链：从国家和区域顶级域名来的外链，会帮助你在那个国家的**Google获得更好的排名。例如，来自.fr的外链会帮助我在google.fr**上获得更好的排名。

101. Link Location In Content: Links the beginning of a piece of content carry slight more weight than links placed at the end of the content.

101. 链接在文章中的位置：出现在越开头的地方越有价值。

102. Link Location on Page: Where a link appears on a page is important. Generally, links embedded in a page’s content are more powerful than links in the footer or sidebar area.

102. 链接在页面中的位置：出现在正文会比其他地方例如侧边栏或者底部更有价值。

103. Linking Domain Relevancy: A link from site in a similar niche is significantly more powerful than a link from a completely unrelated site. That’s why any effective SEO strategytoday focuses on getting relevant links.

103. 外链来源网站的内容相关度：获取外链的网站的内容最好和你本站的内容相关。

104. Page Level Relevancy: The Hilltop Algorithm states that link from a page that’s closely tied to page’s content is more powerful than a link from an unrelated page. 104. 页面相关度：同上，只不过细化到页面层级。

105. Text Around Link Sentiment: Google has probably figured out whether or not a link to your site is a recommendation or part of a negative review. Links with positive sentiments around them likely carry more weight.

105. 外链周围文字的语义：**Google会读取外链周围的文字并判断其语义，例如别人提到你网站，给出网站链接的时候，是负面评论，那Google**自然不会傻乎乎的还认为你的网站很受欢迎。

106. Keyword in Title: Google gives extra love to links on pages that contain your page’s keyword in the title (“Experts linking to experts”.)

106. 外链来源页面的**title：如果你的目标关键词也出现在了外链来源页面的title**标签中，那么也会相应的有加分。

107. Positive Link Velocity: A site with positive link velocity usually gets a SERP boost.

107. 外链增长速度呈上升趋势：这样是好的信号，**Google**会认为你的网站越来越受欢迎。

108. Negative Link Velocity: Negative link velocity can significantly reduce rankings as it’s a signal of decreasing popularity.

108. 外链增长速度呈下降趋势：与上一条相反。

109. Links from “Hub” Pages: Aaron Wall claims that getting links from pages that are considered top resources (or hubs) on a certain topic are given special treatment.

109. 从**“目录页面”**来的外链： Aaron Wall**（国外知名网站** seobook**的创始人）认为：从顶级资源站（或是分类目录）导出的链接会获得极高的评分。**

110. Link from Authority Sites: A link from a site considered an “authority site” likely pass more juice than a link from a small, microniche site.

110. 链接来自权威网站：某个网站链接来自权威网站的加分会比这个网站在其它小网站的链接价值更高。

111. Linked to as Wikipedia Source: Although the links are nofollow, many think that getting a link from Wikipedia gives you a little added trust and authority in the eyes of search engines.

111. 从维基百科来的外链：虽然链接是**nofollow**的，但许多人都认为从维基百科的链接会让你的网站在搜索引擎眼中提升一点点信任度和权威度。

112. Co-Occurrences: The words that tend to appear around your backlinks helps tell Google what that page is about.

112. 关键词外链在不同网站共同出现：这些围绕你外链出现的词往往会帮助**google**更好的识别你所链接到的页面内容是什么。

113. Backlink Age: According to a Google patent, older links have more ranking power than newly minted backlinks.

113. 外链生成的时间：存在时间越久的外链越有价值。

114. Links from Real Sites vs. Splogs: Due to the proliferation of blog networks, Google probably gives more weight to links coming from “real sites” than from fake blogs. They likely use brand and user-interaction signals to distinguish between the two.

114. 从**“真站”来的外链：“真站”**即确确实实有人在好好运营的站，这种站有真实的访问者，良好的内容。而大部分的站群网站和免费博客都属于假站，基本没自然的真实访客，内容也毫无价值，甚至很长时间都不更新。

115. Natural Link Profile: A site with a “natural” link profile is going to rank highly and be more durable to updates.

115. 自然链接**:** 一个网站的外链全部是自然链接那么**google**排名将会很好且持久。

116. Reciprocal Links: Google’s Link Schemes page lists “Excessive link exchanging” as a link scheme to avoid.

116. 互惠链接 : google**链接方案页面指出应避免过多的交换链接出现在网站。**

117. User Generated Content Links: Google is able to identify links generated from UGC vs. the actual site owner. For example, they know that a link from the official WordPress.com blog at en.blog.wordpress.com is very different than a link from besttoasterreviews.wordpress.com.

117. UGC**友情链接：谷歌能够识别从**UGC （用户生成内容）产生的链接与实际网站的所有者链接的区别。例如，他们知道官方**WordPress.com的博客en.blog.wordpress.com产生的链接与xxxx.wordpress.com**上的链接非常不同。

118. Links from 301: Links from 301 redirects may lose a little bit of juice compared to a direct link. However, Matt Cutts says that a 301 is the similar to a direct link.

118. 来自**301定向的链接：一个来自301重定向的链接可能会比直接产生的外链要差一点点，但是Matt Cutts**说过， 301**重定向链接类似于直接链接。**

119. Schema.org Microformats: Pages that support microformats may rank above pages without it. This may be a direct boost or the fact that pages with microformatting have a higher SERP CTR.

119. Schema.org 微格式：包含微格式页面的网站排名往往比页面上没有微格式的网站排名要高 。

120. DMOZ Listed: Many believe that Google gives DMOZ listed sites a little extra trust.

120. 是否被**DMOZ收录：许多人认为, google会给DMOZ**列出的网站一点额外的信任。

121. Yahoo! Directory Listed: The algorithm might also have a special place for the Yahoo! Directory, considering how long it’s been cataloging sites.

121.**是否被Yahoo Directory收录：google算法也同样考虑到雅虎目录，但是，目前站长需要考虑的是要给雅虎多少钱来保证收录时间。**

122. Number of Outbound Links on Page: PageRank is finite. A link on a page with hundreds of OBLs passes less PR than a page with only a few OBLs.

122. 外链来源页面的导出链接数量：如果一个页面上导出链接太多，那么你再去获取外链也价值很小了，除非这个页面有相当高的权重。

123. Forum Profile Links: Because of industrial-level spamming, Google may significantly devalue links from forum profiles.

123. 论坛个人账户资料中的链接：黑帽**SEO及SPAM的猖獗，已经让Google**把这类链接的价值尽量减小了。

124. 外链来源页面的文章长度：一个外链如果来自于一个**1000字内容的页面，显然比来自于一个仅有25**字内容的页面更有价值。

125. 外链来源页面的文章质量：如果文章质量太差，各种语法拼写错误，甚至是伪原创或抄袭的重复内容，那么这个外链的价值相应的也会比较差。

126. Sitewide Links: Matt Cutts has confirmed that sitewide links are “compressed” to count as a single link.

126. 全站链接：**Matt Cutts说过Google**往往把全站每个页面都会出现的链接仅仅算作一个链接。

127. 某个关键词的点击率：当某个关键词的点击率（即**CTR，Click Through Rate**）不错时，那么这个网页在该关键词的排名可能会有一定上升。

128. 所有关键词的平均点击率：整个页面（或者整个站点）的搜索点击率会作为一个基于真实的用户层级的对你网站排名的积极因素。

九、网站上**webspam**因素

177. Panda Penalty: Sites with low-quality content (particularly content farms) are less visible in search after getting hit by a Panda penalty.

177. 搜索引擎规则惩罚：早在熊猫算法之后，搜索引擎已能够很好的识别伪原创和低质量内容了。

178. Links to Bad Neighborhoods: Linking out to “bad neighborhoods” — like pharmacy or payday loan sites — may hurt your search visibility.

178. 链接到恶邻：链接到假药或者高利贷、博彩类网站会伤害到你网站的搜索引擎可见度。

179. Redirects: Sneaky redirects is a big no-no. If caught, it can get a site not just penalized, but de-indexed.

179. 重定向：欺骗性重定向是一个很大的禁忌。如果被抓住了，它可能不只是惩罚一个网站排名或**PR**，而是从搜索引擎中完全被删除索引。

180. Popups or Distracting Ads: The official Google Rater Guidelines Document says that popups and distracting ads is a sign of a low-quality site.

180. 弹窗或漂浮广告：**Google搜索质量评分指南**文件指出，弹出式广告和漂浮广告是一个低质量网站的标志。

181. Site Over-Optimization: Includes on-page factors like keyword stuffing, header tag stuffing, excessive keyword decoration.

181. 网站过度优化：包括对页的因素，如关键字堆叠，**标题标签堆砌，过度的关键词描述**。

182. Page Over-Optimizaton: Many people report that — unlike Panda — Penguin targets individual page (and even then just for certain keywords).

182. 页面过度优化 ：很多人报告说，不像熊猫 ，企鹅算法之后，**Google**开始针对单个页面（甚至只是某些关键字） 。

183. Ads Above the Fold: The “Page Layout Algorithm” penalizes sites with lots of ads (and not much content) above the fold.

183. 广告过多：在**“页面布局算法”**惩罚的网站有很多是因为广告过多，多到大于内容的。

184. Hiding Affiliate Links: Going too far when trying to hide affiliate links (especially with cloaking) can bring on a penalty.

184. 隐藏的**友情链接**：太过隐藏的友情链接，特别是那些高度隐藏友链的网站会被惩罚。

185. Affiliate Sites: It’s no secret that Google isn’t the biggest fan of affiliates. And many think that sites that monetize with affiliate links are put under extra scrutiny.

185. 联盟网站：这已经不是什么秘密，但**Google并不会对交换友链视而不见。在Google链接政策**里已经写得很清楚。

186. Autogenerated Content: Google isn’t a big fan of autogenerated content. If they suspect that your site’s pumping out computer-generated content, it could result in a penalty or de-indexing.

186. 自动生成的内容：**Google**完全能够识别伪原创内容，结果可能会导致受罚或取消索引。

187. Excess PR Sculpting: Going too far with PR sculpting — by nofollowing all outbound links or most internal links — may be a sign of gaming the system.

187. 多余的**PR**修饰： nofollowing**所有的出站链接或大部分内部链接（这一点并不太被笔者认同，因为维基百科全部是nofollow外链和dofollow的内链，而Google非常喜欢维基百科类的网站，这也符合PageRank算法原理）**

188. IP Address Flagged as Spam: If your server’s IP address is flagged for spam, it may hurt all of the sites on that server.

188. IP**地址标记为黑帽：如果您的服务器的IP地址已被标记为黑帽，它可能会伤害所有该服务器上的网站。**

189. Meta Tag Spamming: Keyword stuffing can also happen in meta tags. If Google thinks you’re adding keywords to your meta tags to game the algo, they may hit your site.

189. 黑帽元标记：关键字堆砌也有可能发生在**meta标签。如果谷歌认为您要添加关键字到您的meta**标签是欺骗算法，他们可能会打击你的网站。

十、非页面**webspam**因素

190. Unnatural Influx of Links: A sudden (and unnatural) influx of links is a sure-fire sign of phony links.

190. 激增的**非自然链接**：链接突然（和不自然的）激增很可能会被认定为作弊。

191. Penguin Penalty: Sites that were hit by Google Penguin are significantly less visible in search.

191. 算法惩罚：被**Google**算法惩罚的网站往往看不到多少搜索结果。

192. Link Profile with High % of Low Quality Links: Lots of links from sources commonly used by black hat SEOs (like blog comments and forum profiles) may be a sign of gaming the system.

192. 高百分比的低质量链接 ：如博客评论，博客软文等等低质量的关键词链接被视为一个黑帽ＳＥＯ的标志。

193. Linking Domain Relevancy: The famous analysis by MicroSiteMasters.com found that sites with an unnaturally high amount of links from unrelated sites were more susceptible to Penguin.

193. 链接域相关性：著名的分析网站**MicroSiteMasters.com**指出，居高不下的不相关网站来的不明外链会被视为作弊。

195. 从相同**C类IP来的链接：从同一IP**服务器的不同网站获得的链接会被认为是不自然的。

197. 人工处罚：以**Matt Cutts为首的Google反垃圾小组是各大黑帽站长的噩梦。（相信不少朋友都经历过PR值从3、4甚至5直接到0**的体验，没有错就是他们干的）。

198. Selling Links: Selling links can definitely impact toolbar PageRank and may hurt your search visibility.

198. 销售外链：卖外链会影响到你网站的**PR**值，更会影响你网站在搜索引擎中的可见度。

199. Google Sandbox: New sites that get a sudden influx of links are sometimes put in theGoogle Sandbox, which temporarily limits search visibility.

199. 谷歌沙盒：新的网站，突然得到如潮涌般的外链很多时候会被**Google放入Google沙盒**中，这暂时限制了搜索关键词后的网站可见度。

200. Google Dance: The Google Dance can temporarily shake up rankings. According to a Google Patent, this may be a way for them to determine whether or not a site is trying to game the algorithm.

200. 排名更新：**Google在每一次排名更新后都会影响到不少的网站。据一份Google**专利指出，他可能是一个用算法来判断网站是否作弊的方法。
